# Summary

Bill Wake coined INVEST (Independent, Negotiable, Valuable, Estimable, Small, Testable) here, and introduced the "a story is a multi-layer cake, slice it vertically" metaphor. It is the source of the "more than a month means I don't understand it" diagnostic and of framing overlap as the most painful form of dependency.
