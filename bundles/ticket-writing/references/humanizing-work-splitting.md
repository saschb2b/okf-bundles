# Summary

The most-referenced modern story-splitting guide. Meta-pattern: find the core complexity, identify the variations, then cut one complete slice. Nine named patterns, plus the rules "favor the split that lets you drop the low-value part" and "favor roughly equal sizes". Includes a downloadable flowchart teams use as a refinement cheat sheet.
