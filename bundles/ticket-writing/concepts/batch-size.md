# Summary

Reducing batch size reduces cycle time, accelerates feedback, lowers risk, and reduces variability in flow (Reinertsen). It is the most-emphasized lever in his work on product development flow. Smaller stories also improve predictability and create momentum.

# Why it matters for tickets

Right-sizing is the cure for [the Boulder](/smells/boulder.md). Achieve it through [vertical slicing](/techniques/vertical-slicing.md) and the splitting toolkits ([SPIDR](/techniques/spidr.md), [story-splitting patterns](/techniques/story-splitting-patterns.md)).
