# Summary

Popularized by Steve McConnell (after Boehm). At initial concept, an estimate can be off by up to four times high or four times low, a sixteenfold spread. It represents the best-case accuracy possible; without deliberate work to resolve unknowns it becomes a "cloud of uncertainty". Time passing is not the mechanism; resolving unknowns is.

# Why it matters for tickets

Explains why early estimates on [Icebergs](/smells/iceberg.md) are unreliable, and why a [spike](/techniques/spike.md) (which resolves unknowns) narrows the cone where merely waiting does not.
