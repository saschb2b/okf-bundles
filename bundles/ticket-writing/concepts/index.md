# Concepts

The mental models that explain why the [smells](/smells/index.md) hurt.

- [Planning fallacy](planning-fallacy.md) - We underestimate our own tasks.
- [Cone of Uncertainty](cone-of-uncertainty.md) - Early estimates are least reliable.
- [Curse of knowledge](curse-of-knowledge.md) - Knowing makes you unable to imagine not knowing.
- [Flow metrics](flow-metrics.md) - Cycle time, throughput, WIP, work item age.
- [Little's Law](littles-law.md) - Cycle time equals WIP divided by throughput.
- [Batch size](batch-size.md) - Smaller batches finish faster.
