# Statement

Average Cycle Time = Average WIP / Average Throughput (equivalently, WIP = Throughput x Cycle Time). The practical consequence: to shorten cycle time at a fixed throughput, limit WIP.

# Why it matters for tickets

The math behind [the Boulder](/smells/boulder.md) and the case for WIP limits and small [batch size](/concepts/batch-size.md). See [flow metrics](/concepts/flow-metrics.md).
