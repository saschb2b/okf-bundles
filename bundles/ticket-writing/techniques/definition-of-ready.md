# Definition

A short shared agreement that an item has stated value, testable acceptance criteria, is small enough, and has known dependencies. It mirrors the Definition of Done. It is not part of the Scrum Guide; it is external and optional.

# The caution

A rigid Definition of Ready becomes a mini-waterfall: a stage gate where "refinement" hands off to "development", which is the large-batch handoff lean flow warns against. Frame it as "we know enough to start and will adapt", not "every question answered in advance".
