# Definition

An ongoing activity (not a mandated Scrum event) where the team turns big, vague ideas into work it can deliver. Budget roughly 5 to 10 percent of each sprint, and aim only for "sufficiently understood", not perfectly specified. Formerly called "grooming" (now a deprecated term).

# In the session

- Run a [Three Amigos](/techniques/three-amigos.md) pass on anything fuzzy.
- [Example Map](/techniques/example-mapping.md) anything you cannot explain in a sentence.
- Split anything bigger than a few days on the spot, using [SPIDR](/techniques/spidr.md) and the [story-splitting patterns](/techniques/story-splitting-patterns.md).
- Make "send it back" a normal, blameless move, driven by the [ready smell-test](/playbooks/ready-smell-test.md).
