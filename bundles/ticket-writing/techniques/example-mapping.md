# The four cards

- **Yellow**: the story.
- **Blue**: a rule or acceptance criterion.
- **Green**: a concrete example of a rule.
- **Red**: an open question nobody can answer yet.

# The built-in smell test

A small group should map a well-understood, well-sized story in about 25 minutes. If they cannot, it is too big or too uncertain. Many red cards mean too much is implicit (see [the Tapper](/smells/tapper.md)); many blue rules mean the story is too big (see [the Boulder](/smells/boulder.md)).
