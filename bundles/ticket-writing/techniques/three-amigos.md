# Definition

Three lenses look at a story together: business (what problem?), development (how to build it?), and testing (what could break?). The output is agreed scope and a shared idea of done, captured before the story is "ready". It is deliberately lightweight and timeboxed (commonly 30 to 60 minutes).

# Use it to

Defeat [the Tapper](/smells/tapper.md), the single-cursed-author problem. Often run inside [backlog refinement](/techniques/backlog-refinement.md), frequently as an [Example Mapping](/techniques/example-mapping.md) session.
